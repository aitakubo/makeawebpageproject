How to Grow Succulents

You may have bought a succulent from Trader Joe's after hearing they're the easiest thing to take care of!


Only for it to end up in a pile of mush by the end of the week.

Succulents may not be as easy as you might think, but fear not; with the right knowledge, they'll be thriving with minimal maintenance!

The 3 Big Rules:

  • Lots of Sunlight
  • Quick-Draining Soil
  • Adequate Watering

In general, these are the three main rules that will pertain to almost every succulent. The most important factor is the species of your plant. Care may differ from plant to plant, like growing seasons, watering needs, etc, so use google to your advantage! Reddit is also a great source for identifying your plant. Try joining the succulents subreddit!
